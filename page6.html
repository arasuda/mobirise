3D Modeling

Show showing the reason why people donate

There are many different reasons why people donate blood. This piece is to showcase some of the reasons people donate blood, in hopes this would convince people at hospitals to donate

Three Stage Plan

1

Attention

The coloured background will attract people to look at the poster and notice the 3D model and the information that is presented in it

2

Interaction

This information will give men and women logical and emotional reason to why people donate blood

3

Donation

Putting the thought of blood donation into the viewers minds,  may lead to them wanting to find more information from their doctor

3D Models

ways to show my data on fusion 360

First Model

This design was based on a jigsaw, where the inside pieces of information could be removed, to encourage interactivity

Second Model

This design was based on a solid blood bag design, where the data would be showcased as equidistant bars in the centre

Third Model

This design was based on the previous model however the blood bag was replaced with a heart, as the heart is the source of blood

The purposes of the models above were to create a unique way of presenting that data that I have gathered. I chose not to use a blood bag as the 3d model as the edges would be able to be smoothed, thus causing the edges not to be too jagged. The heart design was the most suitable shape to hold the data I have gathered

This exhibition would be set up in Sheffield Hallam University in the Creative Design building, as part of an open exhibition on blood donation. However, if it was on its own, it would be placed in the student health services as the data relates to current students.

www.shu.ac.uk/study-here/health-and-social-care

STUDENT NURSES

The data that will be presented in this exhibition is personal information; thus this exhibition will only be shown in colleges/universities/science exhibition in Sheffield 

Since this information was gathered from different people in Sheffield, This means that the information would only be relevant to that city.  If this information were to go into a science exhibition more data would have to be acquired from different towns/cities to make either national infographics or different regional infographics

This is the final concept draft that I chose before amending the text and the amount of blood in each of the blood bags. The data in this design will be shown in 2 different ways, The first will be as a bar chart that will be incorporated into the 3D model, and the second will be a percentage that will be shown in each of the blood bags.
